Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a very important recipe to my family and I. We love to eat it on cold days when there is nothing better than pasta to warm you up.

Today, I will be teaching you all how to make this lasagna my family loves so dearly. And no, we didn't just buy it from Costco and heat it up or anything.

    Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • tomato sauce
  • protein of choice (I prefer ground beef)

    Steps

  1. Soak lasagna noodles in salted water.
  2. Season sauce with salt, pepper, and oregano.
  3. Defrost ground beef and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Begin layering sauce, beef, and noodles in that order.
  5. Place in oven at 400 degreed Farhenheit for 40 minutes until done.
