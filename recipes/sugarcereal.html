Sugar Cereal

Description

So, this recipe is exactly what it sounds like. No surprises except for the sugar at the bottom of your bowl at the end of already sugary cereal delight!

Today, I will be teaching you all how to make the breakfast of my childhood dreams, sugar with a side of cereal.

    Ingredients

  • cereal of your choice
  • white sugar
  • milk

    Steps

  1. Pour cereal of your choice into a bowl.
  2. Sprinkle sugar over the cereal.
  3. Pour milk over the sugar-sprinkled cereal.
