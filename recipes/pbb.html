Peanut Butter and Banana Toast

Description

Peanut butter and banana toast (or PBB) is an unrivaled breakfast, and I am not saying this as an opinion.

Today, I will be teaching you all how to make the breakfast of my dreams, and yes, it sounds like a Tyler song.

    Ingredients

  • one slice of bread
  • one banana
  • peanut butter (crunchy or smooth, doesn't matter)

    Steps

  1. Toast the slice of bread.
  2. Slice the banana into thin 1/4in slices.
  3. Remove toast from oven and spread peanut butter generously on bread.
  4. Place banana slices on top of peanut butter.
  5. Place in oven at 400 degreed Farhenheit for 3 minutes or until very warm.
